TV Sleep Timer · Kors Motion

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 4, 2026

Overview

TV Sleep Timer does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All settings are stored locally on your device and never leave it.

This application is developed by Kors Motion (Sergej Korsakov), based in Switzerland. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Permissions Used

  • 🔒
    Device Administrator

    Used to lock the screen when the sleep timer expires. Primary method on NVIDIA Shield and Android TV. Can be deactivated at any time in device settings.

  • ⚙️
    Modify System Settings

    Used to temporarily adjust screen timeout to trigger sleep mode. The original value is always restored after the timer executes.

  • 🪟
    Draw Over Other Apps

    Used to display the countdown overlay and clock widget on top of other content while you watch TV.

  • 🔄
    Receive Boot Completed

    Used to restore scheduled timers after the device restarts, so your schedule continues working automatically.

Weather Data

If you enable the clock widget with weather display, weather data is fetched via a Kors Motion proxy server. Only a general city-level location is used — no precise GPS coordinates are collected or stored.

Third-Party Services

TV Sleep Timer does not include any advertising SDKs, analytics tools, or user tracking. No data is shared with third parties. The app is distributed via Google Play — Google's own privacy policy applies to the Play Store itself.

Children's Privacy

This app does not collect data from anyone. There is no account system, registration, or data collection of any kind.
